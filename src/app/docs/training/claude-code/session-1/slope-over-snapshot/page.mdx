# Slope Over Snapshot

We measure learning trajectory, not a point-in-time skill level.

1. Where you are at the end of Session 1 matters less than how fast you're learning
2. The strongest fluency signals come from iterating and pushing back on outputs, not from reaching for the most tools
3. Practice logs track your trajectory — what you tried, what you abandoned, how your approach evolved
4. By Session 5, you'll review your own growth arc and share what changed
